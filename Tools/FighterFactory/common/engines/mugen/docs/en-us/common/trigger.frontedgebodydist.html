Gives the distance between the front of the player (as determined by the front edge of his width bar) and the edge of the screen.

Format: FrontEdgeBodyDist

Arguments:

  • none

Return type: float

Error conditions: none

Example:

trigger1 = FrontEdgeBodyDist < 30
; Triggers if the front of the player is within 30 pixels of the edge of the screen in front of him.
